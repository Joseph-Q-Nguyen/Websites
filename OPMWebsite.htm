One Punch Man

This anime is a remake of a webcomic create by a guy known as ONE. The anime is directed by Shingo Natsume. This anime is about a man that train so hard to become strong that he a becomes bald. He very strong to the point where he is able to take out monsters with one punch.

Saitama

The main character in this anime is Saitama. He used to live a boring life as an average business man but then one day he wanted to become a super hero so he trained by doing 100 push-ups, 100 sit-ups, 100 squats, and running 10 km every day.

Threat Levels

There are four threat levels in the anime (there are actually 5 in the manga) that show how dangeroussomething might be. These threat levels also give heroes the choice to either show up or not. If the threat level is low, some of the stronger heroes won't come, but when the threat level is larger, than the stronger heroes must stop it.

God
A threat endangering the survival of humanity in general.
Dragon
A threat endangering multiple cities.
Demon
A threat endangering a whole city or its facilities.
Tiger
A threat endangering the lives of a great number of people
