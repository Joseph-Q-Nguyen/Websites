Byakuya Kuchiki

Ichigo Kurosaki

Ichigo Kurosaki

Ichigo Kurosaki is part human, part Soul Reaper. His father was a Soul Reaper and his mother was a human. When he was a kid, he could see ghosts and he helps them. One night, Rukia came to the human world because there was a hollow attacking. She could not defeat it, so she asked Ichigo for help. He agreed to help because his family was in danger. Rukia gave Ichigo her Soul Reaper powers and Ichigo was able to defeat the hollow. This will forever change his life. He saves world multiple times because it is his duty as a Soul Reaper.

Rukia Kuchiki

Rukia Kuchiki
